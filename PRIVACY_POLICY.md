# Privacy Policy for WorkTools

**Last updated:** January 28, 2025

## Overview

WorkTools is a browser extension that enhances your workflow on JIRA and GitHub. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

## Data Collection

**WorkTools does NOT collect, transmit, or share any personal data.**

All data stays locally on your device and is never sent to external servers.

## Data Storage

The extension stores the following data locally using Chrome's built-in storage API:

| Data | Purpose | Storage Location |
|------|---------|------------------|
| JIRA Base URL | To open JIRA tickets quickly | Local (chrome.storage.sync) |
| Recent Tickets | To show your recently accessed tickets | Local (chrome.storage.sync) |
| Feature Toggles | To remember your preferences | Local (chrome.storage.sync) |

This data syncs across your Chrome browsers if you're signed into Chrome, using Google's secure sync infrastructure.

## Permissions

The extension requires these permissions:

- **storage**: To save your settings locally
- **activeTab**: To reload the current tab when you toggle features
- **Host permissions (JIRA & GitHub sites)**: To inject UI enhancements on these specific sites

## Third-Party Services

WorkTools does NOT:
- Use analytics or tracking services
- Make requests to external servers
- Share data with third parties
- Display advertisements

## Data Security

All data remains in your browser's local storage. We have no access to your data.

## Your Rights

You can:
- Clear all stored data by removing the extension
- View stored data via Chrome's developer tools
- Toggle features on/off at any time

## Changes to This Policy

Any updates to this privacy policy will be posted on this page with an updated date.
